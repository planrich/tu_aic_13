Quick reminder

  1. Extract data from Yahoo Finance
  2. Ask human workers to determine sentiment of data
  3. Build a knowledge base of company and product mentions
  4. Allow our users to query it and obtain meaningful information

System components

Brand new user interface

(Dynamic) pricing

  • Make easy tasks cheap, and difficult ones more rewarding
  • Price is 1 cent per 100 letters
  • Bonus is one quarter of a price, and activates after 5 days

Management of bad workers

  • Workers collect negative points if their opinion differs from majority
  • \(p \) - percentage of most common answer
  • \(p \ge 80\%\): good gets +3, bad gets -2
  • \(50\% < p < 80\%\): good gets +1, bad gets -1
  • \(p \le 50\%\): everyone gets -1

Task cleaning

  • Depending on a size of workforce, there may be many tasks left
  • Old tasks should be removed after some time
  • Runs manually and removes tasks that are older than 10 days
