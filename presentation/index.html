Data example 2

Title: Surfing central banks in a benign 'QE trap'

Challenges

  • How do we identify which paragraphs have mentions?
  • How do we correctly aggregate aliases? (i.e. Hewlett-Packard & HP)
  • How do we correctly aggregate product lines? (i.e. iPhone & iPhone 5s, iPad & new iPad)

First aproach - Use a predefined set of company names

  • Use a predefined list of companies and products
  • Crowd source analysis only for text fragments that contain mentions

Rationale

  • Crowd sourcing every paragraph looking for mentions is expensive
  • Identifying company and products aliases is hard
  • Collections of relevant companies and products exists
